03 Greedo

The Wolf of grape street

American Musican/Rapper 03 Greedo (Real Name "Jason Jamal Jackson") comes from the Watts Jordan Downs projects in Los Angeles. He has been incarcerated since 2018 and was jus released on parol january 13 2023, after 5 years behind bars.

He became popular off of songs such as "Substance" "Disco Shit" and "Floating" in 2017-2020 despite being a prolific musician since 2013.

He has multiple stage names and has a unique outlook on music and life due to his tough upbringing and his experiences. He has inspired a whole wave of musicians and helped progress hip hop with his unique sound and voice.

He made a statement after being released from jail stating he is hoping to continue to move the genre forward and continue to make different and better music
